Jesse's Boxing Studio

Building a community around the world through boxing!

Services

Boxing Trainer

Jesse offers individual as well as group lessons. He has experience teaching a wide variety of age groups, from childern to seniors and can adjust to any skill level from amateur to professionals.

Volunteer Work

Jesse is commited to making his idea of building an international community through boxing a reality. He regularly holds free classes and seminars for less fortunate children around the world.

Ambassador

During his free time, Jesse also serves as the Japanese Travel Ambassador for Machu Picchu. As an ambassador, he regularly gives talks throughout about the great experinces one can have at an amazing historical site like Machu Picchu. He also holds a yearly charity event in Machu Picchu for the local children call Santa's Project. During the event there are opportunities for children to enjoy games, arts and crafts and even receive a gift from Jesse personally.
